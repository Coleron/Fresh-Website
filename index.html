Side Walk Labs - Toronto

Ottowa




Sidewalk’s team of urbanists and technologists are exploring ideas for improving urban life that could become components of Sidewalk Toronto. Sidewalk Labs is designing a district in Toronto’s Eastern Waterfront to tackle the challenges of urban growth, working in partnership with the tri-government agency Waterfront Toronto and the local community. This joint effort, called Sidewalk Toronto, aims to make Toronto the global hub for urban innovation.
